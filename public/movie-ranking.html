The Best Movies According to Paul

My top 3 movies of all time

Casablanca

It's a great love story of two people loving each other very much,
but unfortunately, they are divided by war
and their strong morals and priciples.

Gone with the wind

Another great love story.

Blade Runner

A classic sci-fi movie, about advanced technology, androids,
and the impossible love between a human and an android.
